# DocComponentStudio — Feedback & Support

Welcome to the official feedback and support hub for **DocComponentStudio**.

This repository is dedicated to:
- Reporting bugs
- Requesting new features
- Asking questions
- Sharing suggestions to improve the experience

Your input helps shape the future of DocComponentStudio, and we appreciate every report.

---

## 🐞 Report a Bug
If something isn’t working as expected:

1. Go to the **Issues** tab.
2. Select **New Issue**.
3. Choose **Bug Report**.
4. Fill out the details (screenshots encouraged).

Please include:
- What you were doing
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Steps to reproduce the issue

---

## 💡 Request a Feature
Have an idea that would make DocComponentStudio better?

1. Open a **Feature Request** issue.
2. Describe the feature and why it matters.
3. Add examples or mockups if helpful.

---

## ❓ Ask a Question
If you’re unsure about something or need clarification, open a **Question** issue.

---

## 📸 Attach Screenshots
Screenshots or screen recordings help us diagnose issues faster.

---

## ❤️ Thank You
Your feedback directly improves DocComponentStudio.  
We appreciate your time and contributions.
